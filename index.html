Curriculum Vitae
I'm a research scientist at ByteDance working on fairness, transparency, security, and privacy in machine learning systems.

I obtained my Ph.D. degree in Computer Science from University of Chicago, advised by Prof. Ben Zhao and Prof. Heather Zheng. Previously, I received three B.S. degrees in Computer Science, Mathematics, and Statistics from University of Minnesota-Twin Cities.
